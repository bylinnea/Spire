# Privacy Policy for Spire

*Last updated: May 2026*

## Overview

Spire is a plant care tracking app. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

**The short version: Spire does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

---

## Data stored on your device

Spire stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Plant names, photos, and care schedules you enter
- Care history and health log entries
- App preferences and settings
- API keys you provide (stored in Android's encrypted storage)

This data never leaves your device except as described below under Third Party Services.

---

## Third party services

AI features in Spire are **completely optional** and disabled by default. If you choose to enable them, you will need to provide your own API keys from the following services:

### Anthropic (Claude AI)
- Used for: plant care recommendations and AI Plant Doctor
- When enabled, photos and plant descriptions you submit are sent to Anthropic's API
- This is billed directly to your Anthropic account
- Anthropic's privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

### PlantNet
- Used for: photo-based plant identification
- When enabled, photos you submit are sent to PlantNet's API
- PlantNet's privacy policy: https://plantnet.org/en/privacy-policy/

**These services are only contacted when you explicitly initiate an AI action in the app. No data is sent automatically in the background.**

---

## Data not collected

Spire does not collect:

- Personal information (name, email, location)
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data that leaves your device without your explicit action

---

## How to delete your data
 
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of it.
 
**To delete all your data:**
 
1. Open Spire
2. Tap the settings icon (⚙) in the top right
3. Scroll to the bottom to the danger zone section
4. Tap **delete all data**
5. Confirm twice — this permanently deletes all plants, care logs, and settings
Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data from your device.
 
Since Spire does not collect or store any data on external servers, there is no additional deletion request needed.
 
---

## Backup files

When you use the export feature, a backup file is created locally on your device. This file is only shared if you explicitly choose to share it using the system share sheet. We never receive or have access to your backup files.

---

## Children's privacy

Spire does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not target children.

---

## Accuracy disclaimer

AI-generated content in Spire, including care recommendations, plant identification,
and pet safety information, is provided as a guide only and may not always be accurate.

- Plant care recommendations should be treated as a starting point, not professional advice
- Pet safety information is based on AI responses and may be incomplete or incorrect. 
  Always verify with a veterinarian before introducing plants to pets
- Plant identification may be incorrect, especially from unclear photos

Spire and its developer are not responsible for any harm resulting from acting on
AI-generated information in the app.
